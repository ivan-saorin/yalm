# dict5 — Closed Dictionary (5-year-old level)

> **Rule**: Every word in every definition must itself be defined in this dictionary.
> Basic inflections (-s, -ed, -ing) are forms of the base word.
> 50 entries. Each entry: definition + 3 usage examples.

---

## GROUND WORDS (self-referential by necessity)

**thing** — all that is.
- "a dog is a thing"
- "the sun is a thing"
- "a sound is a thing"

**is** — tells what a thing is.
- "a dog is an animal"
- "the sun is hot"
- "water is a thing"

**a** — one of a thing.
- "a dog"
- "a big ball"
- "a good person"

**not** — not yes is no. not good is bad. not big is small.
- "not big is small"
- "not hot is cold"
- "not good is bad"

**it** — the thing. a thing you see or name.
- "the dog is big. it is good."
- "the sun is hot. it is up."
- "the ball is small. it is on the place."

---

## STRUCTURAL WORDS

**and** — this with that.
- "a dog and a cat"
- "big and small"
- "hot and cold"

**or** — this or that, not this and that.
- "a dog or a cat"
- "big or small"
- "yes or no"

**the** — that one thing.
- "the dog"
- "the big ball"
- "the sun is up"

**to** — a move to a place. a give to a person.
- "give the ball to you"
- "move to the place"
- "the dog moves to the cat"

**of** — a part of a thing. the color of the ball.
- "part of the dog"
- "the name of the cat"
- "the color of the sun"

**in** — a thing is in a thing.
- "the water is in the place"
- "the cat is in the place"
- "the food is in the ball"

**on** — a thing is on a thing.
- "the ball is on the place"
- "the cat is on the ball"
- "the food is on the place"

**has** — a thing has a thing in it or on it or with it.
- "the cat has a color"
- "the dog has a name"
- "the person has a dog"

**can** — a thing can do a thing.
- "a dog can move"
- "a person can see"
- "a cat can eat food"

**with** — a thing and a thing, not one.
- "the dog with the cat"
- "the person with the ball"
- "food with water"

**this** — the thing that is to you. not that.
- "this dog is big"
- "this is good"
- "this ball is the ball"

---

## QUESTION / RESPONSE WORDS

**what** — you see a thing. what is it? what is the name?
- "what is the dog?"
- "what color is the ball?"
- "what is that?"

**yes** — it is. not no.
- "is the dog big? yes."
- "can you see? yes."
- "is the sun hot? yes."

**no** — it is not. not yes.
- "is the cat big? no."
- "is the dog a cat? no."
- "is the sun cold? no."

**you** — the person that sees this and feels this. not the dog, not the cat. you.
- "you can see"
- "you can feel"
- "you can eat food"

---

## PROPERTY WORDS

**big** — not small. the sun is big.
- "a big dog"
- "a big ball"
- "the sun is big"

**small** — not big. the cat is small.
- "a small cat"
- "a small ball"
- "a small part"

**good** — not bad. you feel good.
- "the food is good"
- "the sun feels good"
- "a good dog"

**bad** — not good. you feel bad.
- "bad food"
- "the water is bad"
- "a bad sound"

**hot** — you feel hot. the sun is hot. not cold.
- "hot water"
- "hot food"
- "the sun is hot"

**cold** — you feel cold. not hot. water is cold.
- "cold water"
- "cold food"
- "the water is cold"

**up** — not down. the sun is up.
- "the ball moves up"
- "the sun is up"
- "see up"

**down** — not up. water moves down.
- "the ball moves down"
- "the cat moves down"
- "water moves down"

## ACTION WORDS

**see** — you see a thing that is not on you. you see the dog. you see the sun.
- "see the cat"
- "you can see the ball"
- "see the color of the sun"

**feel** — you feel a thing on you or in you. you feel hot. you feel good.
- "feel the water"
- "you feel cold"
- "you feel bad"

**move** — a thing is in a place. the thing moves. the thing is not in that place.
- "the dog moves"
- "the cat can move"
- "the ball moves down"

**make** — you do a thing and a not-thing is a thing. you make food. you make a sound.
- "make a big ball"
- "the person makes food"
- "make a sound"

**eat** — you eat food. the food moves in you. you eat to feel good.
- "the dog eats food"
- "the cat eats"
- "eat the food"

**give** — you has a thing. you give it. you not has it. a person has it.
- "give the ball to the dog"
- "give food to the cat"
- "the person gives water"

**live** — to live is to move, eat, and feel. an animal lives. a person lives.
- "the dog lives"
- "the cat lives in a place"
- "a person lives"

**do** — to make a thing. you do it.
- "what can you do?"
- "do this"
- "a dog can do it"

## CATEGORY WORDS

**animal** — a thing that lives. it can move. it can eat. it can feel.
- "the animal moves"
- "an animal eats food"
- "an animal can see"

**person** — an animal that can make things and give names.
- "a person can see"
- "a person has a name"
- "a person can make food"

**food** — a thing that an animal can eat. it makes you feel good.
- "the dog eats food"
- "good food"
- "hot food"

**water** — a thing you can see and feel. it moves down. an animal can eat water.
- "cold water"
- "the water moves down"
- "water is in the place"

**color** — what you see on a thing. red is a color.
- "the color of the ball"
- "what color is the cat?"
- "a thing has a color"

**place** — a thing that has things in it. a dog can live in a place.
- "a big place"
- "a small place"
- "the animal lives in a place"

**sound** — a thing you can feel. you can not see a sound. an animal can make a sound.
- "a big sound"
- "the dog makes a sound"
- "a bad sound"

**part** — a thing that is of a thing. not all, one of a thing.
- "a part of the dog"
- "a part of the ball"
- "the up part"

**name** — a sound that is of one thing. "dog" is the name of an animal.
- "the name of the cat"
- "you has a name"
- "what is the name?"

**one** — not many. one thing. one dog.
- "one cat"
- "one ball"
- "one person"

**all** — not one, not part. all things. all of it.
- "all the food"
- "all animals"
- "all of the water"

## ENTITY WORDS

**dog** — an animal. it can make sound. it can live with a person.
- "the dog eats food"
- "the dog moves"
- "a good dog"

**cat** — a small animal. it can move with not-sound. it can live with a person.
- "the cat is small"
- "a cat eats food"
- "the cat and the dog"

**sun** — a big hot thing that is up. you can see it. you can feel it. it makes things hot.
- "the sun is up"
- "the sun is big and hot"
- "you can see the sun"

**ball** — a small thing. it can move. you can give it. a person can make a ball move.
- "the ball moves up"
- "a big ball"
- "give the ball to the dog"
