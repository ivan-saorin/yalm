## What "is a" tells you

When you see "is a" it tells you what a thing is.

A dog is an animal. This tells you: dog is in the animal group.
A cat is an animal. This tells you: cat is in the animal group.
A person is an animal. This tells you: person is in the animal group.

A dog is a thing. An animal is a thing. All things that live are animals.
But not all things are animals. A ball is a thing. A ball is not an animal.
The sun is a thing. The sun is not an animal.

When a thing is a thing, you can see it this way:
dog is a animal is a thing.
cat is a animal is a thing.
What is a dog? A dog is an animal.
What is a cat? A cat is an animal.
What is an animal? An animal is a thing.

---

## What "is" tells you

When you see "is" with not "a" it tells you how a thing is.

The sun is hot. This tells you: sun has the hot feel.
The sun is big. This tells you: sun has the big thing.
A cat is small. This tells you: cat has the small thing.

But "is" and "is a" are not the same.
"A dog is an animal" tells you what it is.
"The sun is hot" tells you how it is.

---

## What "can" tells you

When you see "can" it tells you what a thing can do.

A dog can move. This tells you: dog has the move do.
A person can make things. This tells you: person has the make do.
An animal can eat. This tells you: animal has the eat do.
An animal can feel. This tells you: animal has the feel do.

What can a dog do? A dog can move. A dog can eat. A dog can feel. A dog can make sound.
What can a cat do? A cat can move. A cat can eat. A cat can feel.
What can a person do? A person can make things. A person can give names.

If an animal can eat, and a dog is an animal, a dog can eat.
If an animal can feel, and a cat is an animal, a cat can feel.

---

## What "has" tells you

When you see "has" it tells you what a thing has in it or on it or with it.

A dog has a name. This tells you: name is a part of dog.
A cat has a color. This tells you: color is a part of cat.
A thing can has parts. The parts are of the thing.

---

## What "not" tells you

When you see "not" it tells you the opposite.

Not big is small. Not small is big.
Not hot is cold. Not cold is hot.
Not good is bad. Not bad is good.
Not up is down. Not down is up.
Not yes is no. Not no is yes.

If the sun is hot, the sun is not cold.
If a cat is small, a cat is not big.
If the sun is big, the sun is not small.

A dog is not a cat. A cat is not a dog.
A dog is an animal. A cat is an animal. But a dog is not a cat.
Two things can be in one group and not be the same thing.

A ball is not an animal. A ball is not hot. A ball is not cold.
You do not know what color a ball is. The name does not tell you.

---

## What "of" tells you

When you see "of" it tells you a part or a thing that is with a thing.

The color of the ball. This tells you: the ball has a color.
The name of the dog. This tells you: the dog has a name.
A part of the animal. This tells you: the animal has parts.

---

## What "in" and "on" tell you

When you see "in" it tells you a thing is in a thing.
When you see "on" it tells you a thing is on a thing.

The cat is in the place. This tells you: the place has the cat in it.
The ball is on the place. This tells you: the place has the ball on it.
Water is in the place. This tells you: the place has water in it.

---

## What "to" tells you

When you see "to" it tells you where a thing moves or what you give.

Give the ball to the dog. This tells you: the ball moves to the dog.
Move to the place. This tells you: you go to the place.

---

## How to know and how to not know

You know what a thing is when the name tells you.
You know a dog is an animal. The name tells you.
You know a cat is small. The name tells you.
You know the sun is hot. The name tells you.

You do not know what you can not see in the name.
What color is a dog? You do not know. The name does not tell you.
What is the name of the cat? You do not know. The name does not tell you.
Is water good? You do not know. The name does not tell you.
Is the ball hot? You do not know. The name does not tell you.

When you do not know, you say "I do not know."
This is good. To not know is not bad.

## How one thing tells you about a thing

A dog is an animal.
An animal is a thing that lives.
An animal can move, eat, and feel.

So: a dog is a thing. A dog can move. A dog can eat. A dog can feel.
You see this: dog is a animal is a thing.
What is of animal is of dog.

A cat is a small animal.
So: a cat is a thing. A cat can move. A cat can eat. A cat can feel.
And: a cat is small.

A person is an animal that can make things and give names.
So: a person is a thing. A person can move. A person can eat. A person can feel.
And: a person can make things. A person can give names.

---

## What you can not do

You can not say a thing is what it is not.
A ball is not an animal. You can not say the ball lives.
The sun is hot. You can not say the sun is cold.

You can not give a name to a thing that has no name in what you see.
The dog has no color name. You can not make one.

You can not say you know when you do not know.
This is the most good thing: to say "I do not know" when you do not know.
